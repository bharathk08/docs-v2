## New features

**Azure DevOps two-way sync** — Full bi-directional sync between Test Management and Azure DevOps, generate test cases directly from work items for instant traceability, and keep sprints, sprint changes, and story edits synced in real time.

**AI-powered defect creation** — AI auto-generates defect summaries and descriptions from test steps, failure logs, and execution context, formatted developer-ready to reduce manual edits during logging.

## Improvements

**Jira dynamic defect form** — The defect form now pulls issue types, custom fields, and required fields straight from your Jira project, validates against Jira's configuration rules before submission, and reflects Jira config changes automatically.
