Use Copilot to delete test steps you no longer need. You can delete a single step directly from the step row or select multiple steps and delete them at once. This article discusses deleting test steps using Copilot.

---

> <p id="prerequisites">Prerequisites</p>
>
> Before you begin, ensure that:
>    - You have created a test case.
>    - You have installed and configured the **Testsigma Terminal**.
>    - You have referred to the documentation on [Launch Copilot](https://testsigma.com/docs/copilot/launch-copilot/).

---

## **Steps to Delete a Test Steps**

1. In the **Copilot** dialog, hover over the step you want to delete and click the ellipsis icon on the right side of the step. 
   ![ellipsis](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/delete_copilot_1.png)

   
2. Click **Delete Step**.
   ![Delete Step](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/delete_copilot_2.png)

3. In the **Delete step?** dialog, click **Delete** to remove the step from the test case permanently. 
   ![Delete](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/delete_copilot_3.png)

---

## **Steps to Delete Multiple Steps**

1. In the **Copilot** overlay, hover over a step you want to delete and click the checkbox to select the step. A floating action bar appears at the bottom of the panel.
   ![Floating bar](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/delete_copilot_4.png)

2. Continue selecting additional steps by clicking their checkboxes. You can also click **Select All** in the action bar to select all steps at once.
   ![Select all](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/delete_copilot_5.png)

3. Once you have selected all the steps you want to delete, click the **Delete** icon in the floating action bar.
   ![Delete](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/delete_copilot_6.png)

4. A dialog box appears. Click **Delete** to delete the selected steps from the test case permanently.
   ![Delete selected steps](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/delete_copilot_7.png)
