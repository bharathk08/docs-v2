While Testsigma Terminal downloads the required files, you may encounter issues due to network connectivity, insufficient disk space, or firewall restrictions. This article discusses how to resolve the issue. 

---

## **Steps to Resolve the Issue**

1. In the **Testsigma Terminal** window, click **Retry Download**. This restarts the download process. 
   ![Retry Download](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/download_failed_1.png)

2. If the issue persists, click **Troubleshoot Download**. 
   ![Troubleshoot Download](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/download_failed_2.png)

3. Click **Download ZIP** to download the installer ZIP files to your device. 
   ![Download ZIP](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/download_failed_3.png)

4. After the ZIP file is downloaded, select **Continue**.
   ![Continue](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/download_failed_4.png) 

5. Select **Select downloaded .zip file** and choose the ZIP file you downloaded.
   ![Download](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/download_failed_5.png)

6. Click **Continue** to resume the installation.
   ![Continue](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/download_failed_6.png)
