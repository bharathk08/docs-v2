In Testsigma, the test execution video will be available once the test execution is complete. This article discusses how to watch the test execution video. 

---

## **Steps to View Test Execution Video**

1. Navigate to **Create Tests > Test Cases > Test Case > Ad-Hoc Runs**  or open test case level reports from **Run Results**.

   ![Test Case Results](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_view_test_execution_1.png)


2. Click on **Watch Video**.

   ![Watch Video](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_view_test_execution_2.png)


3. The video will be played in a popup. 

   ![Video](https://s3.amazonaws.com/website-static-docs.testsigma.com/new_images/projects/Updated_Doc_Images/update_view_test_execution_3.png)
